Services at Tecsol

Solar energy services

We do Solar power Installation and maintenance Services. Our team of experienced technicians will give you professional and quality work. It includes Solar panels, Inverters, Hybrid Inverters, Batteries, Mounting structures, MPPT Charge Controllers, Change Over and Cabling.

Solar Water Pump Installation

TECSOL will help you save Electricity bill, pump water from your borehole/dam or underground tank using a solar water pump. The water supplied by the solar water pump can be used to irrigate crops, water livestock or provide potable drinking water. It's reliable, efficient and cost friendly.
